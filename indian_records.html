Hello,

On this page I publish monthly and all time records tied/set in India by stations managed by the Indian Meteorological Department (IMD).

I look at the anomaly map and rainfall map in the daily report by IMD and find which station might have set a record. Hence, the list is not exhaustive and some records might get missed.

Source

  1. https://mausam.imd.gov.in/index_en.php
  2. https://www.ogimet.com/home.phtml.en
  3. https://dsp.imdpune.gov.in/home_extremes.php
  4. heads up from the meteorologists, enthusiasts, climatologists, record keepers I follow on social media.


This page will be updated monthly.
