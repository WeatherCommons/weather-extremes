# Data Sources

Where the records on this site come from.

## India

- **India Meteorological Department (IMD) — Daily Weather Bulletin** — <https://mausam.imd.gov.in/index_en.php>
- **IMD Pune — Climatological Extremes** — <https://dsp.imdpune.gov.in/home_extremes.php>
- **OGIMET — Synoptic & climate observations archive** — <https://www.ogimet.com/home.phtml.en>

## Methodology

Daily anomaly and rainfall maps are reviewed by record keepers; suspected records are cross-checked against the official station archives above. The list is not exhaustive — entries may be added or revised retroactively.
